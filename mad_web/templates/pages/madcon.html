MAD Conference is an annual student-run developer conference that aims to provide an environment to learn, develop, and grow. The schedule is a mixture of workshops, tech talks, and coding challenges led by industry engineers.

Workshops

At the core of MAD is our drive to help those learn, develop, and grow. These workshops symbolize those ideals and bring the best and brightest from the industry to make you the best you can be.

Project Showcase

MAD gives students to opportunity to show off their projects they've made and to potentially win a prize.

People

This is ultimately a conference, which you can meet new people from industry or from campus to help you change and empower the world.

FAQ

Is this a hackathon?

No, we are a developers conference.

Who can attend?

All UT Austin students are allowed to attend. You will be notified in late January.

How much does it cost?

Nothing, it's completely free.

What should I bring?

Just yourself, your computer, and your conference badge.

Do I need to have experience in mobile or web development?

Nope, there will be courses for both beginners and advanced folks.
